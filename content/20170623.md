mkdir fossil
cd fossil
<p>fossil clone https://自己學號@/mde2a2.kmol.info/wcmg4/index wcm4</p>
<p>然後就會出現password for 自己學號:??????</p>
<p>之後問的y/n,都輸入y</p>
<p>之後再mkdir wd</p>
<p>cd wd</p>
<p>fossil open ./../wcm4</p>
<p>之後把要clone的所有影片複製到wd裡面有_FOSSIL_檔案的再一起</p>
<p>fossil add .</p>
<p>fossil commit -m "add new mp4 </p>
<p>之後問的都輸入y</p>
